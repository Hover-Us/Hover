KickStarter Coming Soon

Hover Us is creating the Real Hoverboards from your dreams! Not a scooter or flying machine, but truly a skateboard like deck that you ride, floating on air. A board powered by a leaf blower? There will be a stretch goal to have the electronics built in, which will enable some really cool features, but require a lot of $ to develop, and build right. Although UL 2272 might not apply, Hover Us will focus on quality and safety.

It would be nice if it used a Lithium-ion battery that is easy to buy, one that fit your leaf blower or other tools. If an Outdoor Power Equipment Manufacturer wants to work with us, please Contact us. Running time is about 10 minutes so using a swappable, easy to find battery is our plan.

Yes it will be expensive, but less than half of what Hendo hoverboards went for. A Hover Us board does not need any special ramp or surface. Ride it on the street, sidewalk, dirt trail... the surface needs to support the weight (not so much on water) and be somewhat airtight.

History

It started March 5th 2014 when I saw the HUVr Tech Hoax and thought, Why not?

There are many how-to leaf blower hovercraft videos like this from 2010:

I watched and researched but couldn't find what I wanted. Several designs have come up since then, like Hendo (Magnetic Field ArchitectureTM) and Lexus (liquid nitrogen cooled superconductors), but nothing like Hover Us.

I started with an oval board shape and began many years of experimenting, designing, building, and testing.

How much weight can it lift? The force on a surface, the ground area under the skirt, is easy to determine with the area and pressure. The pressure of your blower can be measured with a manometer, which is easy to make. You can sort of calculate air pressure with the published speed rating and Bernoulli's equation. I have been developing corporate websites (full-stack ASP.NET) but last year (OMG it's been years) I decided to focus on Hover Us. Special thanks to someone for her incredible support through this time.

The design has seen many revisions and went from a bag skirt, to wall skirt, to bag and finger, but the current version is crazy. There will be more details, hopefully sooner :)   There is a lot of work to build one, but it is not rocket science. Some time after the KickStarter campaign ends, we will release the how-to STEM focused PDF that covers the steps to design and build your own. The Hover Us design and material kits will also be available in the KickStarter.

FAQs

Q: Will it work on water?
A: Unknown and you should not try it. It might be as buoyant as 2 body boards with 20 pounds (9Kg) on top, but hard to balance.
Q: What will it work on?
A: On smoother surfaces it works great. On typical street, sidewalk, and other hard surfaces it does well. Offroad testing is pending.
Q: What is the range?
A: It's not designed for transportation, just to have fun. It will run about 10 minutes on a charge.
Q: When does KickStarter launch? How can I find out more?
A: Check back, I'll be updating this site.
Q: How about multiple leaf blowers?
A: The STEM focused PDF will cover design and material questions including CF/M vs. MPH (CM/M vs. KPH) requirements.
